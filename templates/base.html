This is a deep learning web app designed to predict if a patient has TB/pneumonia using CNN.

It analyzes chest x-rays and provides results. NEGATIVE indicates normal, and POSITIVE indicates pneumonia.

The dataset used for training includes x-ray images from Kaggle. It was utilized to train CNN for accurate predictions.

Pneumonia Severity Information

Pneumonia severity varies depending on factors such as age, overall health, and underlying conditions.

In severe cases, pneumonia can lead to respiratory failure and sepsis. Early detection and treatment are crucial.

Consult a healthcare professional for evaluation and guidance if you have concerns about pneumonia or chest x-ray results.
